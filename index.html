摘要

Abstract

在过去的十年里, 中国互联网用户数量已经超过30万,互联网的繁荣带来了在聊天室, 微博和其他在线场所创造和传播的单词和固定表达方式数量的
激增。

 

互联网在中国为此类表达方式的传播提供了独特的环境,其中许多表达方式具有政治敏感性,因为它仍然是一个相对未经审查的平台,用户可以即时向数百万人传递信息。 这些消息经常传播标准普通话中找不到的俚语:然而,其中一些俚语已经被广泛接受,以至于它们已经被输入了官方的新华社词典。

为了帮助大家更好的感受与理解网络语言的构成方式和特点,本书收集了2021年中国网络流行语,并进行注解。
Over the past decade,China has had more than 300,000 Internet users, and the internet boom has led to a surge in the number of words and fixed expressions created and disseminated in chat rooms,microblogs and other online venues.
The Internet provides a unique environment for the dissemination of such expressions in China, 

many of which are politically sensitive because
it remains a relatively uncensored platform
where users can instantly deliver information to millions of people.

These messages often spread slang that is not found in standard Mandarin: however, some of it has been so widely accepted that they have been entered into the official Xinhua dictionary. According to David Crystal, an English-Chinese economist," the Internet is an extremely diverse medium and a mirror that reflects many aspects of the nature of language."

 

In particular, the World Wide Web provides
a place for almost all styles of language, mostly written and partly spoken.

 

In Wikipedia, the definition of network language is "network language, representing a certain Internet culture, it is widely used in a variety of Internet applications, it comes from a wide
range of sources, many from dialects and colloquialisms, foreign languages, acronyms, harmonics, misplanting, and even to Symbols merge to pictogram effects, and so on, and belong to a mixed language. "

To help you better feel and understand the composition and characteristics of online language, this book collects and annotates Chinese Internet buzzwords for 2021.Wishing you a pleasant reading!

读不懂的缩写
suō xiě

字体输入作为网络聊天最基本的形式,需要通过键盘或触屏将字符输送到对方的显示屏上,打字速度肯定难与思维同步, 也远非直接对话那样方便,故以简约高效的字母替代汉字就成为网民聊天首选,它的内涵的丰富程度已经超过正规文字表达模式。

如今,似乎一切词语都可以使用缩写,这也造成了一定的交流障碍,因此我们常常能在网上看到许多人的疑惑和愤怒:“这到底什么意思？” “这种词也有必要用缩写吗?“

touch screen.

Therefore,it is only necessary to replace Chinese characters with simple and efficient letters. It has become the first choice of netizens for chat, and its richness of connotation has exceeded the formal textual expression mode.
